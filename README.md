## 🚀 Project Mission

To eliminate the friction between "doing business" and "managing business." This repo houses the scripts, logic, and frameworks required to maintain audit-proof records, optimize tax liabilities, and manage daily operations without the manual overhead.

## 🛠 Included Systems

### 1. The Expense Architect (Mobile-First Utility)

A lightweight, HTML/JS-based expense tracker designed for field use.

* **Purpose:** Immediate data capture for meals, mileage, and business operating expenses.
* **Key Feature:** Built-in CSV export for seamless integration with tax software/accountants.
* **Why it matters:** Eliminates the "shoebox of receipts" problem by forcing the categorization of expenses at the moment of purchase.

### 2. The 2026 Mileage Logic

* **Current Rate:** Optimized for the 2026 IRS standard rate of **72.5 cents per mile**.
* **Utility:** Logic frameworks for tracking mileage that satisfy IRS requirements for "Who, What, Where, Why."
